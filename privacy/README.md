# BPTimer Privacy Policy

This folder hosts the privacy policy for BPTimer, a meditation timer app.

## Live Policy

The privacy policy is hosted at: https://odcpw.github.io/bptimer/privacy/

## Summary

BPTimer stores all meditation session data locally on your device only. No personal information is collected, transmitted, or shared. All data remains private and under your control.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue in this repository.
