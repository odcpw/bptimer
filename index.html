BPTimer Privacy Policy

BPTimer is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

BPTimer stores all meditation session data locally on your device only. No personal information is collected, transmitted, or shared with any external services.

Data Storage

All your meditation sessions, practice records, and statistics remain private and under your complete control. Data is stored using your device's secure local storage.

No Third-Party Access

We do not use analytics, tracking, or any third-party services that could access your data. Your meditation practice remains completely private.

Data Portability

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your information and can delete it at any time through your device settings.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
